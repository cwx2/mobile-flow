Tunnel (WSS)

What is Tunnel mode?

Tunnel creates a TLS-encrypted WebSocket (WSS) server on your computer, allowing your phone to connect securely over any network — even across the internet.

Quick Setup (recommended)

Click "Generate Certificate" to auto-create a self-signed certificate. Set a Bearer Token as your password, then click Save & Apply.

Manual Setup (advanced)

  • Generate your own cert: openssl req -x509 -newkey rsa:2048 -keyout key.pem -out cert.pem -days 365 -nodes
  • Or use a CA-signed certificate (Let's Encrypt, etc.) for production
  • Fill in the absolute paths to your cert and key files below

Security Notes

  • Self-signed certs are fine for dev/home use. Your phone may show a security warning — this is expected.
  • Bearer Token is required — it authenticates your phone to the Agent.
  • For public internet exposure, use a proper CA-signed certificate and a strong token.
  • Private keys are stored in ~/.mobileflow/certs/ with restricted permissions.
